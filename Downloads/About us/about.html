About WIPCO

With over 70 years of expertise, WIPCO delivers advanced industrial coatings and anti-corrosion solutions designed to protect steel structures, machinery, and infrastructure across demanding environments.

Decades of Coating Excellence

Who We Are

Western India Paints (WIPCO) is a trusted manufacturer of high-performance industrial coatings and anti-corrosion paint systems. With over 70+ years of expertise, we specialize in developing advanced coating solutions that protect steel structures, machinery, and industrial assets across demanding environments.

Talk to Our Experts

Our Legacy

Established in 1954, WIPCO has built a strong reputation for delivering reliable and durable coating systems. Over the decades, we have continuously evolved our formulations and technologies to meet the growing needs of infrastructure, manufacturing, marine, and engineering industries.
